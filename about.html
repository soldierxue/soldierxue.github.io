Learning is a lifelong journey with occasional stops.
From customers, to customers.

Jason Xue, an alumnus of Fudan University-Washington University EMBA C22 program, is a Senior Manager - Solutions Architects (SA) at Amazon Web Services (AWS). He currently leads the Strategic Customers SA team, supporting China's top enterprises in their global business expansion. Jason built and nurtured the "E-commerce Industry Expert Squad" with over 50 core members, driving business growth for customers including SHEIN, PatPat, Shinho, Amway, RED, etc. As a certified Amazon Innovation Culture trainer, he has empowered renowned companies like Trip.com, miHoYo, and Geely with innovation capabilities, earning Platinum membership in the Culture Enablement Community. Since joining AWS in 2016, he has successfully transformed from an individual contributor to a senior leader, guiding his team to become trusted advisors for numerous clients in their digital and AI transformation journeys. Jason is passionate about new technologies, community evangelism, and customer service.
